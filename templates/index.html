SONA PLC

SONA PLC is a leading supplier of raw minerals, renowned for its commitment to quality and excellence in the mining industry. Located in Chancho, Oromiya, Ethiopia, SONA PLC has established itself as a trusted provider of a wide range of high-grade minerals used in various industries worldwide.

History and Background

Founded in 2018, SONA PLC has grown from a modest mining operation into a significant player in the global mineral supply chain. With a deep respect for Ethiopia's rich geological resources, the company has dedicated itself to sustainable mining practices, ensuring minimal environmental impact while maximizing resource utilization.

Commitment to Quality

At SONA PLC, quality is paramount. The company employs state-of-the-art technology and rigorous quality control measures to ensure that all products meet the highest standards. Each mineral is meticulously processed and inspected to guarantee purity and performance, making SONA PLC a preferred supplier for various industrial applications.
